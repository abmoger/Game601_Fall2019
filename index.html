My games from GAME 601 - Game Prototyping at American University in Fall 2019

Game Modification: Strategic War
Strategic War adds a new element to the traditional card game of War: attack and defend. Using the same ranking of cards as the original game, this new version of War forces its players to balance their hands as certain cards will be used to attack and others will be used to defend.
PLAY
(Midterm) Non-Digital Game: Timeline
In this card game, each player plays as a specific character, each with special abilities. After a time machine accident, the players are each trapped in a specific time era and must use their abilities, along with their peers', to return to their original time.
PLAY
Visual Novel or Interactive Fiction: Memory
In this interactive fiction game, the player, while daydreaming, returns to a series of memories as they seak one specific one that is just out of their reach.
PLAY
(Final) Digital Game: C-Beam Survivors
In C-Beam Survivors, the player is the captain of a rescue ship that has been dispatched to the site of a recent space battle. They must rescue the surviving escape pods while keeping their ship out of harm's way. Remember, the shareholders are relying on you.
PLAY
